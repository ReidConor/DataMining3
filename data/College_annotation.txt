The college dataset is based on statistics for a large number of US Colleges from the 1995. It has 777 observations on the 6 variables and the class column (isElite). name Ð college nameaccept_rate Ð acceptance rate (number of applications received by the college/number of applications accepted)outstate Ð Out-of-state tuitionEnroll Ð Number of new students enrolledGrad.Rate Ð graduation ratePrivate Ð indicator of private or public universityisElite Ð class label (Elite/not Elite college)
